# General Chemistry · Interactive

**A comprehensive university-level chemistry course**

**Status:** Planned

Part of [Hengzhe's Science Classroom](https://hengzhe-science-classroom.github.io/hub/) · Tier 3 · Undergraduate

## What You'll Learn

- Atomic structure and periodicity
- Chemical bonding and molecular geometry
- Stoichiometry and reactions
- Thermochemistry and thermodynamics
- Chemical kinetics
- Equilibrium and acid-base chemistry
- Electrochemistry

## References

- Peter Atkins, Loretta Jones & Leroy Laverman, *Chemical Principles* (7th ed., W.H. Freeman)
- Steven S. Zumdahl & Susan A. Zumdahl, *Chemistry* (10th ed., Cengage)
- Theodore L. Brown et al., *Chemistry: The Central Science* (15th ed., Pearson)

## Features (when built)

- **Interactive visualizations** — every concept comes alive with draggable, animated demos
- **KaTeX math rendering** — rigorous notation throughout
- **Exercises with hints & solutions** — from computational to proof-based
- **Progress tracking** — saved locally in your browser
- **Zero dependencies** — just open the link and learn
